American Goulash

Description

American Goulash is a classic comfort food dish made with ground beef, macaroni, and a tomato-based sauce. It's hearty, flavorful, and perfect for feeding a crowd!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (15-ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1 (15-ounce) can diced tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 cups elbow macaroni
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. In a large skillet, cook ground beef over medium-high heat until browned. Add onion and garlic, and cook until onion is translucent.
  2. Add tomato sauce, diced tomatoes, Italian seasoning, salt, pepper, macaroni, and beef broth to the skillet. Bring to a simmer, cover, and cook for 15-20 minutes, or until macaroni is tender.
  3. Remove from heat and stir in cheddar cheese until melted. Serve hot and enjoy!
