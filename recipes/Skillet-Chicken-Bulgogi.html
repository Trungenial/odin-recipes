Skillet Chicken Bulgogi

Description

Skillet Chicken Bulgogi is a delicious Korean-inspired dish with tender chicken thighs and a sweet, savory, spicy sauce. Serve it over rice and garnish with green onions for an easy, flavorful weeknight dinner!

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon grated fresh ginger
  • 1 tablespoon gochujang (Korean chili paste)
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 1/2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 green onions, sliced

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, whisk together soy sauce, brown sugar, sesame oil, garlic, ginger, and gochujang.
  2. In a large skillet, heat vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Add chicken and cook until browned and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes.
  3. Reduce heat to low and add the sauce to the skillet. Cook, stirring occasionally, until the sauce has thickened and coats the chicken, about 3-5 minutes.
  4. Remove from heat and garnish with green onions.
  5. Serve over rice and enjoy!
