Skillet Chicken Pasta

Description

Skillet Chicken Pasta is a delicious one-pan meal with tender chicken, pasta, and a creamy tomato sauce. It's easy to make and perfect for a quick weeknight dinner!

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces penne pasta
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1 (14.5-ounce) can diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups baby spinach

Steps

  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium-high heat. Add chicken, salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, basil, oregano, and red pepper flakes. Cook until chicken is browned and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes.
  3. Add diced tomatoes, chicken broth, heavy cream, and Parmesan cheese to the skillet. Bring to a simmer and cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Add cooked pasta and baby spinach to the skillet. Cook, stirring occasionally, until spinach is wilted and pasta is heated through, about 2-3 minutes.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
